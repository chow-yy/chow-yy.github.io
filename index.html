About Me

I am a PhD student at Metropolis University, advised by Professor Jian Yang. My research focuses on reinforcement learning and multi-agent systems, specifically in developing robust AI for complex, real-world coordination tasks. Previously, I received my M.S. from State Tech University. When I'm not in the lab, I enjoy hiking and photography.

Research Interests
